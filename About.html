About +852 Hong Kong

+852 city is a guide for people who have just arrived in Hong Kong and would live in the city for a while.

They can use the website to quickly and effectively learn pop culture in HK as it is not taught in any schools or anywhere.

The aim of +852 city is to help the “outsiders” feel more” insider” and that way we can all feel equal and not have conflict with each other.

This website is for them to utilise the knowledge they gained and use it for their daily lives.

“If all felt equal then there would be no war”
